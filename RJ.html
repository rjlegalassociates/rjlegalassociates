Our Specializations

Civil Law

Civil law concerns the protection of private rights of individuals. We handle a variety of cases, including property disputes, contract issues, torts, and inheritance matters. Our services also extend to providing legal counsel in personal matters such as wills, and family property issues.

Criminal Law

Our criminal law practice covers defense against criminal charges, including theft, assault, white-collar crimes, fraud, and more. We provide expert legal counsel during the investigation process, and represent clients in court, working to achieve the best possible outcome for those accused of crimes.

Arbitration

We offer arbitration services to resolve disputes outside of court. Our team assists in resolving commercial disputes, contractual issues, and employment disagreements through professional arbitration methods, providing an efficient and confidential resolution process.

Divorce

We understand the emotional and financial challenges of divorce proceedings. Our firm provides compassionate and professional legal representation in divorce cases, child custody disputes, spousal support, and property division. We work to ensure that our clients' rights are protected during this sensitive time.
